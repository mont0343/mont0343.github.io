Here are 5 HTML tags that I have learned about in the past few weeks:


'form'
forms provide the backbone to any website with communication between
client and server. We use forms everyday on the websites we interact with,
comments are submitted through forms, Facebook posts, Twitter tweets, etc.
'img'
images make our websites more engaging and helps with the esthetics of the
design. Unlike word or other documents we do not embed images into the
HTML document, we instead link to it.
'mark'
the mark tag allows you to highlight text, as if you were using a highlighter on
your written homework. It is similar to the 'strong' tag in which it helps signify
importance.
'ol'
ordered lists are used when the order of the list is important. For example,
this list would be useful when ranking your top 10 favourite songs.
'strong'
the strong tag allows you to bold certain text to help address importance/catch
the readers attention.
